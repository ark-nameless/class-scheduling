Mission

The Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation - Candelaria, Inc., is a private non-sectarian education foundation with a three-fold function -instruction, research and community servie ofering responsive and alternative programs supportive of national development goals and standards of global excellence.

Vision

The Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation - Candelaria, Inc. shall be a globally competitive university.

Goal

Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation - Candelaria, Inc. shall produce graduates who have research-based knowledge, leadership and managerial skills, and professionalism.

Core Values

We commit to demostrate passion for excellence, principled leadership Personal commitment, loyalty and discipline creativity and critical thinking, respect for human dignity, respect for basic freedom/human rights, excercise of rights andwith responsibility, moral sensitivity, recognition of cultural differences, social justice within the framework of democracy, forward-looking nationalism, sustainable development, Harmony with nature, Self-reliance.
